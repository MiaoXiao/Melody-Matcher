How to play:

Click 'Play Melody' to play a small tune! Your goal is to try to copy that melody, note by note, by playing it back on the piano!
Every melody you successfully copy will get you points. The harder the melody, the more points you get!
You only have a certain amount of time to copy a melody. Successfully copying a melody will rest the timer.
You can change your difficulty or scale from the 'settings' tab.
